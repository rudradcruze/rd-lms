Business Requirements Document
Learning Management System (LMS)

Introduction

The  purpose  of  this  document  is  to  define  the  business  requirements  for  a  Learning
Management  System  (LMS).  The  system  will  provide  a  centralized  digital  learning  platform
where  administrators,  instructors,  and  students  can  manage  and  participate  in  online
educational activities.

The  platform  will  support  course  management,  student  enrollment,  learning  content  delivery,
assignments, quizzes, progress tracking, and communication between instructors and students.

Business Objectives

●  Provide an online learning platform for students and instructors
●  Centralize course and learning material management
●  Enable digital course enrollment and participation
Improve accessibility to educational resources
●
●  Automate assignment and quiz management
●  Track student learning progress
●

Improve  communication  between  instructors  and  students,  admin  to  instructor,  and
student to student.

Business Requirements

BR-01: User Registration and Authentication

The system shall allow users to register and access the platform based on assigned roles.

Business Need

The  organization  requires  a  secure  and  role-based  platform  to  manage  access  and
responsibilities.

Expected Outcome

●  Secure user access
●  Role-based system operations
●  Centralized user management

BR-02: Course Management

The system shall allow instructors and administrators to create and manage courses.

Business Need

The organization requires a centralized structure for managing educational courses and learning
resources.

Expected Outcome

●  Organized course management
●  Consistent course information
●  Easier course administration

BR-03: Student Course Enrollment

The system shall allow students to enroll in available courses.

Business Rules

●  Students may only enroll in active courses
●  Enrollment may require approval based on course settings
●  Enrollment records shall be maintained

Business Need

Students require a streamlined process to participate in learning programs.

Expected Outcome

●  Simplified enrollment process
●  Accurate enrollment tracking & learning accessibility

BR-04: Learning Content Management

The system shall allow instructors to upload and organize learning materials within courses.

Learning Materials May Include

●  Video Lessons
●  PDF Documents
●  Notes
●  External Resources (link/audio/images)

Business Need

Instructors require a centralized method to distribute educational content digitally.

Expected Outcome

●  Organized learning resources
●
●  Consistent content delivery

Improved student access to materials

BR-05: Assignment Management

The system shall allow instructors to create assignments and students to submit assignment
responses.

Business Rules

●  Assignments may include submission deadlines
●  Students may upload files as submissions
●

Instructors may review and grade submissions

Business Need

The organization requires a structured digital assignment workflow.

Expected Outcome

●  Simplified assignment handling
●  Centralized submission management
●

Improved evaluation process

BR-06: Quiz and Assessment Management

The system shall allow instructors to create quizzes and assessments for enrolled students.

Quiz Features

●  Multiple-choice questions
●  Timed assessments
●  Automatic scoring

Business Need

The organization requires online assessment capabilities to evaluate student learning.

Expected Outcome

●  Faster evaluation process
●  Automated grading support
●

Improved monitoring

BR-07: Student Progress Tracking

The system shall track student learning progress within enrolled courses.

Progress Indicators

●  Completed lessons
●  Assignment status
●  Quiz performance
●  Course completion percentage

Business Need

Students and instructors require visibility into learning progress and performance.

Expected Outcome

●
●

Increased student engagement
Improved learning management

BR-08: Communication and Notifications

The system shall support communication between instructors and students.

Communication Features

●  Course announcements
●  Notifications
●  Discussion or messaging support

Business Need

Educational activities require timely communication and information sharing.

Expected Outcome

Improved interaction

●
●  Faster information delivery
●  Better student engagement

BR-09: Administrative Management

The system shall allow administrators to manage users, courses, and platform operations.

Administrative Functions

●  User management
●  Course monitoring
●  Content moderation
●  Reporting and analytics

Business Need

The organization requires centralized operational control over the platform.

Expected Outcome

●  Efficient platform administration
●
Improved operational oversight
●  Better reporting capabilities

Conclusion

The  proposed  Learning  Management  System  (LMS)  will  provide  a  centralized  and  scalable
platform  for  managing  digital  learning  operations.  The  system  is  expected  to  improve
educational  accessibility,  streamline academic workflows, and support effective communication
between students, instructors, and administrators.
